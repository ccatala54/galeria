Welcome

In this gallery, various interpretations of nature are displayed through art.

Here, you can enjoy a small selection of artworks with different monthly themes. This month’s theme is:


WAR

We invite you to reflect on the devastation that war leaves in its wake. Each artwork reflects the invisible scars of those who endure it. The ruins are not only physical but also emotional, marking generations. In these pieces, violence is portrayed not only through weapons but also in the loss of the most essential element: humanity. However, even in darkness, there is a glimmer of hope, a yearning for peace that persists beyond the chaos. War, though ever-present, cannot extinguish the dream of a fairer and more peaceful world.
